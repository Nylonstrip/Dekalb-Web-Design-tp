About Dekalb Web Design

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Dekalb County, Georgia, Dekalb Web Design has been serving businesses with professional web development services for over 10 years. We started with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, affordable web design services that help local businesses thrive in the digital world.

What sets us apart is our commitment to hand-coding every website from scratch. While others rely on page builders and templates, we craft custom solutions that are faster, more secure, and perfectly tailored to your business needs.
